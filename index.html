About Happy Jele Annual Games

WELCOME TO THE OFFICIAL HOME OF THE HAPPY JELE ANNUAL GAMES

The Happy Jele Annual Games is a Yearly Multi-Sporting Event Powered by Legenday South African Soccer Player, Happy Quinton Jele. The event features different Sports Tournaments with a Special Focus on the Main Soccer Event featuring Grass root Teams Specialy Selected by the HJAG Scouting Team.

The Event is aimed at fostering young Talent and creating a Foundation for future Sportsman and Sportswoman.
